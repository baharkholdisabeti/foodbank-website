    About


    Food banks are an essential service that provide food and much needed resources to thousands of Canadians daily. With many Canadians wanting to contribute more to their communities by donating to food banks and community centers, it is important for them to donate resources that will be of great use to their local food banks.

    Food drives are often only able to accept non-perishable food items which, while valuable to food banks, are not enough to provide a balanced diet for families who rely on food banks for their meals. I've Got Extra strives to meet the current needs of food banks, including perishable items such as produce, meat, and dairy products. The site was created to connect the needs of food banks with the resources their community has to offer on an easy-to-use platform. Simply search for your local food bank to see what items are most needed for that week, or filter food banks based on the resources that you are able to donate.
